About Us

Runway Master was developed to address the complexities of flight scheduling and runway allocation. Our platform uses advanced simulation and optimization techniques to ensure efficient airport operations.

Our primary objective is to optimize the allocation of shared resources (runways and aprons) at Airport A, while considering the constraints and variables associated with flight operations.

We use Discrete Event Simulation (DES) to model the operations and resource allocation, and Non-dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm II (NSGA-II) for optimizing resource utilization and minimizing delays.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Runway Master?

Runway Master is a simulation and optimization platform designed for efficient flight scheduling and runway allocation at airports.

How does the simulation work?

We use Discrete Event Simulation (DES) to model flight operations and resource allocation dynamically.

What optimization techniques are used?

We utilize the Non-dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm II (NSGA-II) to achieve multi-objective optimization, balancing various conflicting goals such as minimizing delays and maximizing resource utilization.
